/ Commands

Simple.
Powerful. Fast.

All VidGo commands are slash-based. Type / in any channel where VidGo is present and it'll appear automatically.

/download
Download a TikTok video without watermark in the best available quality. Supports all TikTok URL formats.
Example: /download https://vm.tiktok.com/xxx
/mp3
Extract and download only the audio from any TikTok video as a high-quality MP3 file.
Example: /mp3 https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/123
/info
Get detailed metadata about a TikTok video: title, author, likes, views, duration, and more.
Example: /info https://vm.tiktok.com/xxx
/batch
Download multiple TikTok videos at once by pasting up to 5 links separated by spaces.
Example: /batch link1 link2 link3
/quality
Choose a specific download quality: 1080p, 720p, 480p, or 360p for the video.
Example: /quality 720p https://vm.tiktok.com/xxx
/help
Display the full command list with descriptions, usage examples, and links to documentation.
Example: /help

FAQ

Common questions.

Does VidGo need to be added to my server? +
No! VidGo is a user-installable bot. You add it to your Discord account, not a server. This means you can use it in any DM, group chat, or server you're in — without admin permissions.
Are downloads really watermark-free? +
Yes. VidGo fetches the original video source directly from TikTok's CDN before the watermark is applied, meaning your download is completely clean.
Is there a download limit? +
The free tier allows up to 20 downloads per day per user. For unlimited downloads, you can upgrade to VidGo Pro (coming soon).
Does VidGo store my videos or links? +
No. VidGo processes your request, delivers the file, and immediately deletes all associated data. We don't keep any logs of your downloads.
Can I download private TikTok videos? +
No. VidGo can only download publicly accessible TikTok content. Private videos are inaccessible by design and we respect user privacy settings.
What video formats does VidGo support? +
VidGo downloads videos in MP4 format and audio in MP3 format. These are the most compatible formats for Discord's file system.
